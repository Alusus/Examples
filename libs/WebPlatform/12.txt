onResize: Signals that the widget's dimensions changed.
onMouseEnter: Signals that the cursor entered the widget's region.
onMouseOut: Signals that the cursor exited the widget's region.
onMouseDown: Signals that the mouse button has been pressed.
onMouseUp: Signals that the mouse button has been released.
onMouseMove: Signals that the cursor moved over the widget.
onTouchStart: Signals the start of a touch on devices with touch screens.
onTouchEnd: Signals the end of a touch on devices with touch screens.
onTouchMove: Signals the change of touch positions on devices with touch screens.
onClick: Signals that a mouse click occurred.
